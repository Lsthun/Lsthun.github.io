Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bay St. Louis Campground collects, uses, and shares information when you visit bslcampground.com.

Who We Are

Bay St. Louis Campground operates this website to provide information about campground stays, rates, amenities, nearby attractions, and contact details for reservations.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information through this website. We do not offer customer accounts, online checkout, or an online booking form on this site.

Information may be collected in the following ways:

If you contact the campground by phone, use directions links, or visit third-party services linked from this site, those interactions may involve information processed by your phone carrier, map provider, or the third-party service you choose to use.

When you browse this website, technical information such as pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, browser type, device information, and referral source may be processed for site measurement and security purposes.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this website, which pages are viewed, how visitors arrive at the site, and whether visitors click important actions such as calling the campground or requesting directions.

We collect anonymous usage data in the form of aggregate website usage reports. Google may also process technical data associated with your visit, such as IP address, browser or device information, and cookies or similar identifiers, in order to provide analytics services.

Our Google Analytics configuration is used for website measurement only. Advertising personalization and Google signals are disabled in the site configuration used by this website.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google's data usage page.

Cookies and Consent

If Google Analytics is enabled on this website, a cookie notice banner is presented so you can choose whether analytics cookies and similar measurement technologies may be used on your device.

If you accept, analytics will load and may store or read cookies or similar identifiers for measurement purposes. If you decline, Google Analytics will not be loaded by this site.

You may also control cookies through your browser settings, delete stored cookies, or use browser privacy tools. You can return to your analytics choice on this site by selecting Cookie Preferences.

How We Use Information

We use information collected through the website to:

Measure website performance and visitor interest, understand which marketing sources drive traffic, improve page content and navigation, monitor high-value actions such as phone and directions clicks, and maintain the security and reliability of the site.

Sharing

We do not use this website to sell personal information. Information collected through Google Analytics is processed by Google as our analytics provider, subject to Google's terms and policies.

This website also contains links to external services such as Google Maps and third-party attraction or event sources. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies and practices.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for the period configured within the Google Analytics property for this website, unless a shorter retention period is required by law or operational needs.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to request access to personal information, request correction or deletion, withdraw consent, or object to certain processing activities. The exact scope of these rights depends on the laws that apply to you.

To make a privacy request related to this website, contact Bay St. Louis Campground using the contact information below.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the website, analytics configuration, legal requirements, or business practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
