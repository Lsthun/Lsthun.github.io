Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bay St. Louis Campground collects, uses, and shares information when you visit bslcampground.com.

Who We Are

Bay St. Louis Campground operates this website to provide information about stays, rates, amenities, local attractions, and reservation contact details.

Information We Collect

We collect limited website usage information to understand site performance and visitor interest. We do not provide online account registration, e-commerce checkout, or a booking form on this website.

When you browse this site, information such as page views, approximate geographic area derived from IP address, browser type, device type, and referring source may be processed for analytics and site maintenance purposes.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, including which pages are visited and whether visitors click actions such as calling the campground or requesting directions.

We collect anonymous usage data in aggregate reporting. Google may process technical information associated with your visit, including IP address, browser or device information, and cookies or similar identifiers used for analytics.

This site uses Google Analytics for website measurement only. Advertising personalization and Google signals are disabled in the site configuration.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google's data usage page.

Privacy Notice

This website shows a lightweight privacy notice on first visit to let users know that Google Analytics is in use. The notice does not block use of the website and may be dismissed with a single acknowledgment.

The site stores a small local browser flag to remember that the notice has already been shown so it does not keep reappearing on later visits.

How We Use Information

We use website usage information to measure traffic, understand which sources drive visits, improve page content and navigation, track high- value actions such as phone and directions clicks, and maintain the reliability of the website.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information through this website. Information collected through Google Analytics is processed by Google as our analytics provider, subject to Google's terms and policies.

This website includes links to external services such as Google Maps and third-party event or attraction sites. Those services operate under their own privacy practices.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for the period configured in the Google Analytics property for this website, unless a shorter period is needed for operational or legal reasons.

Your Choices

You may control cookies and stored website data through your browser settings and privacy tools. You can also limit Google Analytics in your browser using Google's available privacy tools and browser add-on options.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the website, analytics configuration, legal requirements, or business practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
